How It Works

1. Escrow Deposit

Lock your tBTC in an OPNet smart contract. Your request goes live on the board anonymously.

2. Helper Accepts

A vetted helper accepts your request. Counterparty identities are securely revealed to each other.

3. The Session

The helper arrives at the designated hospital to provide non-medical companionship and support.

4. Auto-Release

Both parties confirm completion on-chain. The smart contract instantly releases funds to the helper.

Why build this on Bitcoin?

Traditional care agencies act as middlemen, taking up to 40% of what you pay. By executing logic natively on OPNet Bitcoin smart contracts, we eliminate the intermediary.

Our protocol charges a flat 5% platform fee. Helpers earn significantly more, and families pay less. No custody risk, no delayed payouts—just pure, verifiable code.
