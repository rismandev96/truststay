How it works

Four steps. Two people. One chain of trust.

01
Post your request
Describe what you need — someone to stay with your mom at the hospital, help organize prayers, check on dad. Set your fee and duration.
02
Escrow locks USDC
Your USDC goes into an MPC-secured escrow on Base L2. Nobody touches it until both sides confirm. Gas? Under a cent.
03

Happy? Confirm on-chain. Funds release instantly to the fren's wallet. Problem? 3-party arbitration kicks in.

Why put this on-chain?

Traditional care agencies pocket 30-40% of what families pay. Most goes to overhead, not the person showing up at 3 AM.

TrustStay takes a flat 5% protocol fee. Frens earn more. Families pay less. The escrow contract is public, auditable code — not a terms-of-service PDF nobody reads.
