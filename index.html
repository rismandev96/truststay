The Problem

270 million people live
far from their families.

When emergencies hit, physical distance becomes an impossible gap. There's no Uber for human presence.

You can't teleport

Parent hospitalized in Jakarta while you're stuck in Berlin. The next flight is tomorrow morning. Who stays with her tonight?

Agencies take 30-40%

Traditional care services charge insane markups. Most of it goes to overhead — the person who actually shows up at 3 AM sees a fraction.

Zero accountability

Asking a neighbor has no quality guarantee. Informal cash payments have no dispute resolution, no tracking, no proof of service.

Cross-border friction

Sending money internationally for informal care? Currency conversion fees, 3-day delays, and the person on the other end has no incentive to follow through.

0
platforms exist for on-demand
companion services
Care.com does nannies. TaskRabbit does errands. Gojek does rides. Nobody does "sit with my mom at the hospital tonight." This market is completely unserved.
The Solution

Hire a fren. On-chain.

TrustStay is a two-sided marketplace connecting people who need physical human presence with verified frens who get paid in USDC to show up.

🔒

MPC Escrow

Funds locked in a 2-of-3 MPC wallet on Base L2. No single party can move money unilaterally. Not even us.

📍

GPS-Verified

Frens check in at the location with GPS + photo proof. You see their live position and get real-time status updates.

◆

Soulbound Rep

Every completed task mints a non-transferable reputation token. A fren's track record is on-chain, permanent, and public.

💳

Fiat On-Ramp

Credit card, bank transfer, Apple Pay. Users who don't know what a wallet is can still use TrustStay without friction.

How It Works

Four steps. Two people.
One chain of trust.

01

Post a request

Describe what you need — hospital companion, funeral prayers, elderly check-in. Set fee + duration.

02

Escrow locks USDC

Your USDC goes into an MPC escrow on Base L2. Verifiable on-chain. Gas under $0.01.

03

Fren shows up

A verified fren accepts, checks in via GPS, and sends you live updates the whole time.

04

Confirm & release

Confirm completion on-chain. Funds release instantly. Dispute? 3-party arbitration handles it.

MPC Escrow

Trustless payments.
Not "trust me bro."

Every transaction runs through a 2-of-3 MPC threshold wallet. The full private key is never reconstructed at any single point. Server gets hacked? Attacker can't sign without the user's device share.
